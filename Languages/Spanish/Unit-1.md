## Words:

1.  **la manzana** - the apple
2.  **Tú eres** - you are
3.  **él** - he
4.  **ella** - she
5.  **es** - is
6.  **un** - a (masc)
7.  **una** - a (fem)
8.  **niño** - boy
9.  **niña** - girl
10. **hombre** - man
11. **mujer** - woman
12. **come** - eats
13. **comes** - eat
14. **pan** - bread
15. **la leche** - the milk
16. **el agua** - the water
17. **yo** - I
18. **bebo** - drink (1st person)
19. **bebes** - drink (2nd person)
20. **bebe** - drinks (3rd person)

## Sentences:

1.  **Yo como, tú comes, él come.**<br>
    I eat, you eat, he eats
2.  **Yo bebo, tú bebes, él bebe.**<br>
    I drink, you drink, he drinks
