# UNIT-1
## Note:
In German, we capitalize **every** noun.

## Words:
1. Kafee - Coffe
2. Milch - Milk
3. Bitte - Please
4. Und - And
5. Wasser - Water
6. Tee - Tea
7. Brot - Bread
8. Wein - Wine
9. Danke - Thank You
10. Ja - Yes
11. Nein - No
12. Tschuss - Bye
13. Bier - Beer
14. Oder - or
15. Hallo - hello

## Sentences:
1. **Milche und kaffe, bitte. Danke!**

   Milk and coffee, please. Thank you.
